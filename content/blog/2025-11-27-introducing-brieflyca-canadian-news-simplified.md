## What is BrieflyCA?

BrieflyCA is a **daily news summary app** that focuses exclusively on the Canadian landscape. Unlike traditional aggregators that flood your feed with global noise, we use advanced AI to curate and condense local and national stories.

The platform is built around one core philosophy:

> **Get the facts. Skip the fluff.**

We act as a "clarity layer" between complex reporting and your busy schedule, translating dense articles into meaningful, digestible insights that take less than a minute to read.

## Why we built an AI News Aggregator

In 2025, news consumption faces three major crises:
1.  **Information Overload:** There is simply too much content to process.
2.  **Time Constraints:** Most Canadians don't have an hour to read the morning paper.
3.  **Bias & Polarization:** It's hard to find neutral facts.

BrieflyCA solves this by offering **fast clarity**. We don't replace long-form journalism; we make it accessible. By stripping away opinionated adjectives and clickbait headlines, we deliver the core facts of the story so you can make up your own mind.
