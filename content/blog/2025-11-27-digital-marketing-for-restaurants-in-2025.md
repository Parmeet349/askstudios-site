Restaurants in 2025 depend heavily on digital presence. Whether you're a food truck or a dine-in restaurant, customers expect to see you online.

## What Works in 2025

- Short-form food videos  
- Instagram Reels of menu items  
- Google Maps optimization  
- Automated customer reviews  
- High-quality food photography  

## High-ROI Channels

- TikTok for virality  
- Instagram for visual branding  
- Google Business Profile for local SEO  
- Automated newsletters for repeat customers
